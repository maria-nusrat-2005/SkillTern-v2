# Project Overview: Skilltern

Skilltern is an intelligent internship matching and skill gap analysis platform designed to connect students with relevant internship opportunities, streamline the screening process for recruiters, and provide actionable career insights.

## Vision
To become a career intelligence platform that not only connects students with internships but actively guides them toward becoming industry-ready professionals.

## Mission
Provide an intelligent, transparent, and student-centric internship ecosystem that increases successful internship placements through data-driven matching and skill development insights.

## Problem Statement
The current internship landscape suffers from key inefficiencies:
- **For Students:** Difficulty in locating relevant opportunities, leading to blind applications; a lack of understanding of exact qualification requirements; no feedback on rejection; and a lack of awareness of required industry-level skills.
- **For Recruiters:** Inundation with high volumes of irrelevant applications; manual, time-consuming screening and shortlisting processes.
- **For Universities:** Insufficient tracking of internship placement metrics and student employability trends.

## Proposed Solution
Skilltern addresses these issues via:
1. **Intelligent Internship Matching:** Recommending internship openings based on student skills, interests, academic backgrounds, projects, and past experience.
2. **Match Explanation Engine:** Providing transparent, percentage-based match scores highlighting exact reasons for recommendations (e.g. matching skills and alignment).
3. **Skill Gap Analysis:** Detailing specific technologies and experience areas missing from a student's profile for a specific posting, along with suggestions to close the gap.
4. **Platform Features:** Direct application workflows, notifications, bookmarking, and ratings/reviews post-completion.
